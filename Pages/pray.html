Pray For New Economy
The underground lottery fever was a wave of lottery prediction in Hainan since the year of 2000. At that time,the new economy increased the gap between rich and poor. Local people were unable to adapt to this situation. They created many ways to predict lottery, to relieve their anxiety. I have turned the lottery into a temple where people can pray for blessings.
