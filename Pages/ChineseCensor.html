I have collected news from ChinaDaily and ChinaDigitalTime as an archive. ChinaDaily is the official English language website of China, and ChinaDigitalTime is a collection of censored and deleted articles on the Chinese internet.
I put these two opposing articles together to show different vision of China, and in order to see the censored articles you need to turn off your wifi connection.
